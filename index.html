Welcome to Triangle Calculator

A web app that can calculate the area and perimeter of triangles

Area of Triangle

Formula for the area of a triangle is:

A= 1/2 x b x h
    where
  • A = Area
  • b = base length
  • h = height of the triangle

Calculate the area of a triangle

Perimeter of Triangle

Formula for the perimeter of a triangle is:

P= a + b + c
    where
  • P = Perimeter
  • a = side a
  • b = side b
  • c = side c
